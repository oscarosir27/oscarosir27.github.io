WHAT IS SUGARFREE

Sugar-free to me means a diet free of refined sugar

This is the concept of lower calorie alternatives to your favorite sugary treats. These artificial sweeteners are calorie-free, while others come along with a small amount of calories per serving (but those calories can add up if you eat them often). The most popular sweeteners are still those blue, pink and yellow packets, along with the newest sweetener on the block called stevia. HOW DO I FOLLOW A SUGARFREE DIET?

  • Don't eat any refined foods.
  • No fizzy drinks and beverages.
  • Limit any artificial sweeteners.
